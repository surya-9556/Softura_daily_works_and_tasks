Lamborghini Urus Adding to his luxurious collection is another car from the Italian brand, Lamborghini Urus. The five-seater car comes with 4.0-litre Twin Turbo V8 engine that produces a maximum of 641 Bhp of power and 850 Nm of peak torque. The car comes with 8-speed automatic gearbox. Its price begins from Rs. 3.15 Crore and goes up to Rs. 3.43 Crore. Urus is a successor of the Lamborghini LM002 SUV from the 1980s
Lamborghini Aventador SV One of the fastest supercars in the world, Lamborghini is every car enthusiast's dream. Lamborghini Aventador S is the top model in the Aventador lineup and the top model is priced at Rs 5.01 crore. With a 6.5-litre V12 engine, the machine produces a power of 740 bhp and a massive 690 Nm of peak torque. T he car can go up to 200 kmph in just 2.8 seconds. This is one of the flagship models of Lamborghini owned by Patnaik.
